Harold Heist

(GitHub)

Produced by team Vitorebl (Victor Bordo, Lazareena Thaveethu, Blaise Yokoyama and Tony Bach) over one semester for our Software Development class, Harold Heist is a little game that reminds students at Macalester College of their favorite college memory. Even if you are not a student at Macalester, we hope this will still bring a smile to your face. So click on the picture and have some fun!

Tech stack: Java, libgdx
